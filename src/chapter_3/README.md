# Chapter 3: Research and Strategic Planning — AI as Strategic Partner

## Purpose

Chapter 3 transforms market research from a periodic project into a continuous intelligence system. Agentic research agents don't replace strategists — they eliminate the "exhaustion tax" of manual signal collection, synthesis, and report assembly. Strategists focus on interpretation and judgment while agents handle the pre-work.

## Key Concepts

- **Signal Aggregation**: Continuously collecting and normalizing signals from search, social, reviews, competitor activity
- **Cultural Signals**: Detecting shifts in language, identity, and meaning (beyond just behavioral data)
- **Opportunity Scoring**: Multi-dimensional prioritization (velocity × competition × fit × revenue × feasibility)
- **Proactive Alerts**: Surfacing convergent signals before they become obvious
- **Evidence-Backed Strategy**: Every recommendation traceable to specific data sources

## Research Agent Capabilities

1. **Trend Sensing** — Detect rising topics before they peak (structural vs spike vs seasonal)
2. **Competitive Intelligence** — Map competitor messaging, detect strategic shifts
3. **Consumer Sentiment** — Aspect-based emotion analysis with ensemble models
4. **Opportunity Identification** — White-space mapping (unmet intent + low supply)
5. **Scenario Planning** — If-then strategic options with explicit assumptions
